For the croutons:

Ingredients Amount
French baguette sliced thin 1/2
Extra virgin olive oil 3 tbsp
Minced garlic 1 tsp (2 gloves)
Grated parmesan cheese 2 tbsp

For the dressing:

Ingredients Amount
Minced garlic 1 tsp (2 gloves)
Dijon mustard 2 tsp
Worcestershire sauce 1 tsp
Lemon juice 1 tsp
Red wine vinegar 1 1/2 tsp
Sea salt 1/2 tsp (or to taste)
Black pepper 1/8 tsp

For the salad:

Ingredients Amount
Romaine Lettuce 1 large/2 small head(s)
Parmesan cheese shredded/shaved 1/3 cups
Chicken meat ~500 g or to taste

Caesar Salad Recipe

Classic Caesar Salad with homemade croutons and dressing.

Recipe source

Recipe for croutons:

  1. Cut baguette in half lengthwise and slice into pieces and place on baking sheet.
  2. Combine 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil with minced garlic, toss breads with garlic infused oil and 2 tbsp parmesan.
  3. Spread evenly and bake to desired crispness.

Recipe for dressing:

  1. Whisk together minced garlic, dijon, Worcestershire, lemon juice and red wine vinegar.
  2. Whisking while adding oil emulsifies the dressing for a smooth and creamy (not oily) consistency.
  3. Season with 1/2 tsp salt and 1/8 tsp black pepper, or to taste.

Preparation and serving:

Cut up chicken into desirable chunks and fry on a frying pan with spices. Combine all of your ingredients in a large bowl. This recipe makes enough croutons for two full salads so you can easily make another batch.
